How to apply for visa

• Keep Documents Updated: Ensure your student visa, health insurance, and passport are up to date.
• Understand Work Restrictions: Know the limits on working hours for student visa holders in Australia.
• Stay Informed on Visa Changes: Always check for updates on visa policies through official immigration websites.

Study Tips

• Create a Schedule: Planning ahead and organizing your study time helps you stay focused and prevents last-minute stress.
• Active Learning: Engage with the material by summarizing, questioning, and explaining concepts to others.
• Use Breaks Wisely: Take short breaks every 45-60 minutes to refresh

Budget Tips

• Track Your Expenses: Use apps or spreadsheets to monitor where your money goes each month.
• Prioritize Needs Over Wants: Focus on essential expenses like rent, groceries, and transportation.
• Student Discounts: Take advantage of student discounts on transport, entertainment, and groceries.
