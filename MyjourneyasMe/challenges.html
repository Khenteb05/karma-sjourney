Challenges

One of the most significant challenges I faced was adjusting to life in Australia as an international student.
Being away from family and navigating a new academic environment were difficult at first. However, I overcame this
by making connections with my classmates and participating in student groups. I also created a study routine that
helped me stay on track.

Balancing part-time work and studies has also been challenging, but it
taught me time management and prioritization skills that I now
apply to all areas of my life.
